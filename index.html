Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 May 2026

RadioBlink is an ad-free online radio and audio player application developed by PB Lab.

Information collection and use

RadioBlink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

The application does not require user registration and does not collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos
  • Audio recordings
  • Device identifiers

All radio recordings, playlists, favorites, settings, and local audio files remain on your device and are not uploaded to any server operated by PB Lab.

Permissions

RadioBlink may request certain permissions solely to provide app functionality:

  • Internet access – Required for streaming online radio stations.
  • Media / storage access – Required to play local audio files, display album artwork, and save radio recordings to your device.

These permissions are used only for the stated functionality and are not used to collect personal information.

Third-party services

RadioBlink does not display advertisements and does not sell user data.

The app may connect directly to third-party radio stream providers when you listen to a station. Those providers may have their own privacy practices, which are outside the control of PB Lab.

Data storage

All user-generated content, including recorded audio files, playlists, favorites, and settings, is stored locally on your device.

PB Lab does not receive, store, or process this information.

Children's privacy

RadioBlink is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Since RadioBlink does not collect or transmit personal information, no personal data is stored on PB Lab servers.

Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices and locally stored files.

Changes to this privacy policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page together with the updated effective date.
