Privacy Policy

Smart Shopping List is built with your privacy as a first principle — most of your data never leaves your device.

Effective June 27, 2026 Last updated June 27, 2026
01

Introduction

Welcome to Smart Shopping List, developed and published by PB Lab ("we," "our," or "us").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

02

Information We Collect

A — Information You Provide

The app primarily stores your information locally on your device. This may include:

  • User name (used for personalization)
  • Shopping lists and purchased items
  • Categories and custom shop names
  • Purchase history, prices, and spending records
  • Budget settings
  • Photos of bills or receipts

B — Automatically Collected Information

Some information may be collected automatically through trusted third-party services such as Google Firebase, including:

  • Device model and Android version
  • App version and crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics and anonymous analytics
  • Push notification token (used only for delivering notifications)
🔒 We do not use this information to personally identify you.

C — Permissions Used

📍
Location

Used only to detect your country or region so the correct currency can be suggested automatically.

📷
Camera

Used for barcode scanning and capturing receipt images.

🖼️
Photos / Storage

Used to save receipts, backups, exported PDFs, and restore data.

🔔
Notifications

Used for shopping reminders, scheduled notifications, and important alerts.

You may deny any permission at any time. Some features may become unavailable, but the app will continue to function where possible.

03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Managing shopping lists and tracking expenses
  • Generating shopping analytics
  • Suggesting frequently purchased items
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Creating PDF reports
  • Sending shopping reminders and notifications
  • Restoring backups when requested by you
04

Data Storage

📱 PB Lab does not upload your shopping data to its own servers. Most of your information is stored only on your device.

This includes shopping lists, purchase history, settings, categories, and receipt images.

If you choose to use the backup or export feature, your data may be stored in your selected cloud storage (such as Google Drive) or shared by you using Android's native sharing features.

05

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to help protect your information.

While we strive to safeguard your data, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

06

Data Sharing

🚫 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information is shared only in these circumstances:

  • When required by applicable law
  • With trusted service providers (such as Google Firebase) necessary for app functionality
  • When you explicitly choose to share shopping lists, PDF reports, or backup files
07

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

Google Play Services Firebase Analytics Firebase Crashlytics Firebase Cloud Messaging Google ML Kit iText7

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google services process data.

08

Data Retention

Because most of your information is stored locally on your device, you control how long it is kept.

You may delete your shopping data at any time from within the app or by clearing the app's data through your device settings.

09

Children's Privacy

Smart Shopping List is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take appropriate steps to delete it promptly.

10

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • View your stored information within the app
  • Edit your shopping data at any time
  • Delete your shopping history
  • Remove receipt images
  • Export or restore your data
  • Revoke app permissions through Android Settings
  • Clear all app data from your device at any time
11

International Users

If you use the app outside your country, your information will continue to be handled according to this Privacy Policy.

12

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website, together with an updated "Last Updated" date.
