Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

PB Lab ("we", "our", or "us") operates the DayPilot mobile application ("App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Create

DayPilot allows you to create and manage:

  • Tasks
  • Recurring tasks
  • Reminders
  • Schedules
  • Habits
  • Notes (if applicable)
  • Pomodoro timer settings
  • App preferences

This information is stored locally on your device using Android's Room Database and DataStore.

We do not upload your tasks or schedules to our servers.

Usage Information

If Firebase Analytics is enabled, anonymous usage information may be collected to help improve the App, such as:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Country or region (approximate)
  • Screens viewed
  • Feature usage
  • App crashes and performance information

This information does not include the content of your tasks, reminders, or schedules.

Push Notification Information

If notifications are enabled, the App may use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver reminder notifications.

To provide this service, Firebase may process technical identifiers such as:

  • Firebase Installation ID
  • Device token

These identifiers are used only for delivering notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display and manage your tasks and reminders
  • Schedule local notifications
  • Deliver reminder notifications (where applicable)
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Understand how features are used
  • Fix bugs and crashes

We do not use your information for advertising personalization.

3. Data Storage

Most of your information is stored only on your device. Examples include:

  • Tasks
  • Reminders
  • Habits
  • Schedules
  • Pomodoro settings
  • App preferences

If you uninstall the App, this locally stored information will generally be removed unless you have created your own device backup.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

Google Play Services

Used for:

  • App distribution
  • App updates
  • Ratings and reviews
  • In-app services provided by Google

Firebase Analytics

Used to understand App usage and improve the user experience.

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

Used for delivering reminder notifications.

These services are operated by Google and are subject to Google's own Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

Depending on the features you use, DayPilot may request permissions such as:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to display reminder notifications.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – to restore reminders after the device restarts.
  • Other permissions only when required by specific features.

The App requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share the contents of your tasks, reminders, or schedules with third parties.

Anonymous technical information may be processed by Google services (such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Cloud Messaging) to provide analytics and notification functionality.

7. Data Retention

Your locally stored data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete it within the App, or
  • Uninstall the App.

Analytics information collected by Google services is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

However, no method of electronic storage or Internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

DayPilot is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
