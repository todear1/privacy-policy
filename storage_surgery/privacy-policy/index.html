Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026 Last Updated: June 29, 2026
TL;DR

Storage Surgery analyzes your device storage entirely on-device. Your personal files are never uploaded, collected, or shared. Only anonymous crash reports and usage stats are sent to Firebase to help us improve the app.

01

Information We Collect

A. File Metadata & Storage Information

To provide its core functionality, Storage Surgery analyzes your device's storage locally. The app may access information about files and folders on your device, including:

  • File names, sizes, and paths
  • File types and categories (Images, Videos, Audio, Documents, APKs, Archives, Others)
  • Storage usage statistics
Important: The app does not collect, upload, transmit, or store the contents of your files on our servers. All storage analysis is performed entirely on your device. File operations (delete, move, copy) are executed only after you explicitly initiate them.

B. Usage Data & Analytics

We use Google Firebase services to help improve the quality and reliability of Storage Surgery. Anonymous information may be collected, including:

  • Device model and Android version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g. most-used features)

This information does not include the contents of your personal files.

C. Device Identifiers

Firebase may generate anonymous identifiers — such as the Firebase Installation ID — for analytics, diagnostics, and notification-related services.

02

Android Permissions

MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — All Files Access

Essential for the app's primary functionality: scanning device storage, analyzing disk usage, categorizing files, detecting large files and folders, generating storage statistics, providing TreeMap visualizations, and helping you manage storage efficiently.

This permission is never used for advertising or marketing purposes.

INTERNET

Used only for sending anonymous crash reports and analytics to Firebase, and for checking updates distributed through Google Play.

03

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide storage analysis and file management features
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • Improve the overall user experience
  • Send notifications, if you choose to enable them
04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share anonymous diagnostic and analytics data with trusted third-party service providers (such as Google Firebase) to help maintain and improve the application. We never share the contents of your personal files.
05

Third-Party Services

Storage Surgery may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Google Firebase Analytics Firebase Crashlytics Google Play Services
06

Data Security

All file scanning and storage analysis are performed locally on your device. Any analytics or diagnostic information transmitted to our service providers is protected using industry-standard encryption.

07

Data Retention

Since your personal files are never collected by us, we do not retain them. Anonymous analytics and crash reports collected through Firebase may be retained according to Google's data retention policies.

08

Children's Privacy

Storage Surgery is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

09

Your Rights

Because your files remain on your device, you retain complete control over your data. You may:

  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Disable notifications through your device settings
  • Limit analytics collection where supported by your device or Google settings
